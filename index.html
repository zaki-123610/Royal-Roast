Master the Art of Brewing

Learn to make the perfect cup every time

Pour Over Method

1.Boil water and let it rest for 30 seconds

2.Rinse filter with hot water

3.Add coffee grounds (1:16 ratio)

4.Pour water in circular motion to saturate
grounds

French Press

1.Heat water to 200°F (93°C)

2.Add coarse ground coffee (1:15 ratio)

3.Pour water and stir gently

4.Place lid and steep for 4 minutes

5.Press plunger down slowly

6.Serve immediately

AeroPress

1.Insert filter and rinse with hot water

2.Add fine ground coffee (1:13 ratio)

3.Pour water and stir for 10 seconds

4.Insert plunger and press gently

5.Dilute with water if desired

6.Enjoy your smooth coffee!
